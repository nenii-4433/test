Return & refund policy

Last updated: June 2026

7-day returns

You may return unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached within 7 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Eligible items

  • Items must be in original condition with no signs of wear
  • Printed tees should be unworn; tags must remain attached
  • Exchanges are available for size or colour (subject to stock)

Non-returnable

  • Items worn, washed, or damaged after delivery
  • Items returned without tags or in unsellable condition

How to start a return

Contact us at 0336 3301661 or visit our offices in G-11 or G-9, Islamabad with your order number. We will guide you through the return or exchange process.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.
